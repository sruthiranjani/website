Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

WithMore Gifts ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you place an order, create an account, or contact us, we collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment information.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with our website, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.

Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and store your cart preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process orders and send order confirmations
  • Deliver products and provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Shipping partners for delivery purposes
  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal data.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferences and improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@withmoregifts.com or call 880748412.
